About This Project

A comprehensive business management system built for grain dealers to digitize and streamline their day-to-day operations. The platform covers everything from stock tracking and customer management to financial reporting and expense monitoring.

Designed to give dealers full visibility into their business with real-time analytics, profit/loss tracking, and detailed transaction histories — all in one place.

Features & Functionality

Everything a grain dealer needs to run a smarter business

Dashboard & Analytics

Real-time overview of sales, stock levels, revenue, and expenses with visual charts and key business metrics.

Stock Management

Track grain inventory in real time — add stock, monitor quantities, manage grain types, and get low-stock alerts.

Customer Management

Maintain complete customer profiles, purchase history, outstanding balances, and contact information in one place.

Payment Records

Log and track all incoming payments, view full payment history per customer, and manage outstanding dues.

Expense Management

Record and categorize business expenses, track spending patterns, and monitor cost vs. revenue over time.

Grain Type Management

Define and manage multiple grain types with individual pricing, units, and category-level stock tracking.

Purchase & Sales Tracking

Record every buy and sell transaction with quantity, price, and profit margin calculations automatically applied.

Waste Management

Track grain wastage, record losses, and factor waste into profit/loss calculations for accurate financial reporting.

Reports & Analysis

Generate detailed financial reports, profit/loss summaries, stock movement reports, and customer-wise analytics.
