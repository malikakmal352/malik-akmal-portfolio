Housing Society Management System

2026
Django • Angular • DRF
Web + Android App
About This Project

A comprehensive Housing Society Management System designed to digitize and streamline society operations. It includes a web portal for administration and an Android app for members and department users.

The system centralizes all society operations, improving efficiency, transparency, and record management across all departments.

View Live Demo

Features & Functionality

Comprehensive tools for complete society management

Plot Management

Complete plot allocation, status tracking, ownership management, and transfer handling with detailed records.

Member Management

Full member registration, profile updates, CNIC verification, contact details, and membership records.

Billing & Payments

Automated bill generation, payment tracking, financial records, and comprehensive billing history.

Complaint Management

Ticket system for residents to raise issues, track resolution status, and maintain complaint history.

Announcements & Notifications

Broadcast important updates, notices, and announcements to all members instantly via web and mobile.

Role-Based Access Control

Multi-role access for admins, departments, and members with permission-based features and security.

Security Management

Visitor tracking, gate pass management, and security alerts for enhanced society safety.

Department Management

Manage multiple departments, assign workers, track tasks, and monitor department performance.

Reports & Analytics

Comprehensive financial reports, member analytics, and dashboard insights for informed decisions.
